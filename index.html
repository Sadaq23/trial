About SCADER

SCADER is dedicated to transforming agriculture in Somalia through innovation, education, and sustainable practices. Our team of experts works tirelessly to build resilience in the agricultural sector, creating opportunities for economic growth and food security.

Since our establishment, we have trained over 5,000 farmers, implemented sustainable farming techniques across 10,000 hectares of land, and developed innovative solutions to address the unique challenges faced by Somali agriculture.
